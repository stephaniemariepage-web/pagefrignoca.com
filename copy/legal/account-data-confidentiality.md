# Account Data Confidentiality Undertaking

**Status: DRAFT. Not legal advice. Have a lawyer review before this gates any real upload.**
Click-to-agree (clickwrap) text for the "Score your own data" upload flow. One-way:
the operator of pagefrignoca.com is the party making the confidentiality promise.

---

## Plain-language summary (shown above the checkbox)

You are about to score an export of your own CRM **account** records. Two things to know:

1. **Your data stays in your browser.** Scoring runs client-side. Account records you
   load are processed on your device and are not transmitted to, stored by, or seen by
   pagefrignoca.com or anyone operating it.
2. **Accounts only.** This tool is built for account-level records (company name, domain,
   country, industry, revenue, and similar). Do not upload contact or lead records, or any
   file containing personal data such as individual names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Tick the box to confirm you have read this and agree to the terms below.

> ☐ I have read and agree to the Account Data Confidentiality terms, and I confirm my file
> contains account-level business data only, with no personal data.

---

## Terms

1. **Scope.** "Your Data" means the CRM account export you load into this tool. You agree to
   load account-level business records only and to exclude contact records, lead records, and
   any personal data.

2. **Client-side processing.** Your Data is processed entirely within your browser on your
   device. It is not uploaded to a server, transmitted over the network, logged, retained, or
   accessed by the operator. Closing or refreshing the page clears it from memory.

3. **Confidentiality.** To the extent the operator ever receives any of Your Data (for example,
   if you send it directly during a support exchange), the operator will treat it as
   confidential, use it only to provide the scoring result, and not disclose it to any third
   party or use it for any other purpose.

4. **No warranty on the result.** The readiness score is a directional diagnostic generated by
   a published framework. It is provided as is, with no warranty, and is not a guarantee about
   the quality, accuracy, or fitness of Your Data for any purpose.

5. **Your responsibility.** You confirm you are authorized to use Your Data with this tool and
   that loading it here does not breach any agreement or law applicable to you.

6. **Governing law.** [Jurisdiction to be set on review, e.g. the operator's state of residence.]

## Notes for review (delete before publishing)

- Confirm the client-side claim is true in the shipped build. Clause 2 is the load-bearing
  promise; if any telemetry, error reporting, or analytics could capture record contents, this
  text is inaccurate and must change.
- A one-way undertaking is used deliberately. If the framework or scoring internals ever need
  protecting in the other direction, switch to a short mutual form, but that slows the
  click-to-agree feel.
- Clause 1 scoping (no personal data) is what keeps this out of GDPR/CCPA processor territory.
  Keep the accounts-only constraint enforced in the UI, not just stated here.
- Consider a one-line link to a fuller privacy statement if the site adds analytics later.
